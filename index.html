Kayla Ballantyne

Sometimes it was the white caps dancing on the waves, or the sparkling smooth water that made me feel delighted to see another morning. I am from a place where we savor the sunrise and drink in the sunset: my home the Gem of the Antilles, Saint Vincent & the Grenadines. I would often sit and watch the sea gulls diving either in their misery or success as they encircled their rivals in poorly made row boats, tossing their tattered nets into this other universe hoping for a catch in order to support themselves for another day. I feel blessed, as reflect on these memories of my past life; I appreciate it much more now from a distance.
